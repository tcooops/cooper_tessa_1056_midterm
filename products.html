PRODUCTS



LAGER

Leghorn Lager


Ideal for sunny days, or anytime, really! The Leghorn Lager is a light and refreshing beer, enjoyed by beer drinkers of all walks of life. Nice and light, our Leghorn Lager sits at 4 IBU and 4.5% ABV.





IPA

Free Range IPA


The Free Range IPA is a smooth and easy drinking full-flavoured beer. A subtle hint of refreshing citrus flavours will delight your tastebuds. The Free Range IPA sits at 68 IBU and 6.2% ABV.





RED ALE

Rhode Island Red


Our Rhode Island Red Ale is a beautiful red colour and is full flavoured, hoppy, and buttery with a crisp finish. The Rhode Island Red has an ABV of 5.0% and the IBU sits at 23.





STOUT

Silkie Stout


Named after the cutest chickens in all the land (seriously, look up the Silkies! We promise your heart will melt. This Stout is just as smooth and soft as its namesake. It's an American Stout and sits at 7.3% ABV with 50 IBU. Also an excellent choice of beer to cook with!
