About Us

Our roots are in... eggs!

Gray Ridge Brewing started back in 1934 before craft beer was “cool”. We are still a family owned and run brewery! Our little family farm was always an egg and chicken farm until 1930 when it burned down. The Gray family built the farm back up and decided to turn it into a brewery!



We started selling our bottled beer out of our farm house. Word quickly spread and we became very successful back then. We remain successful now and attribute it all to our secret original family recipe!



Our brew masters have been raised on the brewery-farm their whole lives and live and breathe to bring you the best beers season after season, year after year. Check in often to see what our brew masters have in store next!



We are proud to be leaders in the industry when it comes to sustainability and eco friendly practices. We bring all returned bottles back to our facilities in Ridgetown, Ontario where they go through a rigorous cleaning and sanitization treatment.



You can find us on tap and in cans at many bars and pubs across Ontario. You can find a selection of our canned and bottled beers at your local Beer Store, Beer Store Retail Partner, or LCBO! You can also purchase cans, bottles, and growlers at our Ridgetown brewery.



Drop in weekdays between 12 and 6pm, and weekends between 11 and 9pm for a guided brewery tour and tasting every hour on the hour*! Or relax with a cold beer poured in our farmhouse taproom. Dogs welcome!







* If bringing a group of 10 or more to any given brewery tour, please book in advance through our contact formhere. We are always happy to accomodate large groups!
